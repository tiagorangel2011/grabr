When you tap a Grabr link and consent to data collection, we may collect a range of information. The following are the types of information we may collect:

  1. Browser Information: We gather details about the browser you use to access the link, including its type, version, and language preferences.
  2. IP Information: We acquire IP address-related information, which encompasses the host domain, IP address, reverse DNS, ASN (Autonomous System Number), ISP (Internet Service Provider), country name and code, region name and code, city, postal code, continent name and code, latitude, longitude, metro code, timezone, and the date and time of access.

  4. Operating System Information: We gather information about your device's operating system, including whether it is a mobile device and the name of the operating system it utilizes.
  5. Referrer Information: We collect information about the webpage you visited before accessing the shortened link. This information assists us in understanding how users discover and engage with the link.

In addition to the aforementioned categories, we may also collect other relevant information. To learn more about our data collection practices, please visit our homepage.
